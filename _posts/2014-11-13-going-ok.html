Nothing about living aboard is as hard as I'd imagined it would be.

The boat needs more maintenance than I'd thought it would.

We need a lot more dinghy than we've got in order to actually go anywhere.

We went outside the inlet last Sunday and it was the most exciting ride I've ever had in a boat.  Large standing and breaking waves in all different directions.  Looking up at the bow one moment and then ducking from the spray it makes coming back down the next.

Good times!
