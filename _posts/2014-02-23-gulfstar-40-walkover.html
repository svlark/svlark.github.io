--- layout: post title: Gulfstar 40 walkover date: '2014-02-23T21:24:00.002-07:00' author: Jacob . tags: - front-page modified_time: '2014-03-14T23:00:48.931-06:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-6400085873105072120.post-7180016051527454648 blogger_orig_url: http://www.svlark.com/2014/02/gulfstar-40-walkover.html --- 1982 Gulfstar 40, Cocoa, FL, $59,000

Liked the center cockpit design and the easily accessible engine room. In great condition, especially for the price.

Did not like how the aft companion way spit you out towards the back of the boat. It felt isolated and too separated from the rest of the boat. The split design made the boat seem smaller with two small living areas, instead of one larger one.
